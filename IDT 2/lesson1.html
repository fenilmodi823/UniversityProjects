Lesson 1: Introduction to Astronomy

Welcome to Lesson 1! Explore the fundamental concepts of astronomy.

Overview

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, such as stars, planets, comets, and galaxies, and the phenomena that occur outside Earth's atmosphere. In this lesson, you will delve into the basics of astronomy and gain a foundational understanding of the universe.

Key Topics

  • The Solar System and its components
  • Types of celestial bodies
  • The concept of light years

Interactive Activities

Engage in interactive simulations to visualize the movements of planets, understand the concept of orbits, and explore the vastness of the universe.
