Simulation 2: Day and Night Cycle

Explore the Earth's day and night cycle through this simulation.

About the Simulation

Simulation 2 illustrates the rotation of the Earth and its impact on the day and night cycle. Discover how sunlight interacts with the planet, creating the phenomena of sunrise and sunset.

Interactive Features

  • Control the speed of Earth's rotation
  • Observe changes in sunlight distribution
  • Visualize the day and night regions

Instructions

Follow the on-screen instructions to manipulate parameters and observe the dynamic Earthly phenomena.
