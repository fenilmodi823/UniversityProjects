Simulation 1: Orbits and Gravity

Experience the influence of gravity on celestial bodies.

About the Simulation

Simulation 1 allows you to visualize the effects of gravity on orbiting bodies. Explore how gravitational forces govern the motion of planets, moons, and other objects in space.

Interactive Features

  • Adjust the mass of celestial bodies
  • Alter the eccentricity of orbits
  • Observe changes in gravitational forces

Instructions

Follow the on-screen instructions to manipulate parameters and witness the impact of gravitational forces on celestial orbits.
